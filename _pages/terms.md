## Privacy Policy

The privacy of my visitors is important to me. This page explains what data may be collected when you browse this site and how that information may be used.

I do not sell personal information. If you contact me directly, I will only use the information you provide for communication related to your message.

### Log Files

Like many other websites, this site may use standard server logs to understand traffic and maintain the site. The information in these logs can include:

* Internet Protocol addresses (IP)
* Types of browser
* Internet Service Provider (ISP)
* Date and time stamp
* Referring and exit pages
* Number of clicks

All of this information is not linked to anything that is personally identifiable.

### Cookies and Web Beacons

This site may use cookies or similar technologies from analytics and advertising providers to understand usage and improve the site experience.

If Google AdSense or other third-party advertising services are enabled on the site, those providers may use cookies to serve and measure ads. This site does not directly control third-party cookies. You should review the privacy policies of the relevant providers for more information about how they use data and how to opt out.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your web browser options. Instructions for doing so can be found on the specific web browsers' websites.

#### Google Analytics

Google Analytics may be used to understand how visitors engage with this website. It reports aggregate website trends and usage patterns. You can read the [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).
